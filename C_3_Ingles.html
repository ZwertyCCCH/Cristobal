📖 READING

Why do animals laugh and make friends?

Scientists believe that some intelligent animals laugh and have fun with each other.

Think about: What sounds do chimpanzees make when they play?

📌 EXAM TIP:
First, read the title. This will help you understand what the text is about. Then read quickly from start to finish.
🐒 AMAZING FACTS

Animals that laugh and play

🐀 Rats

Rats play and "laugh" like humans! They make high-pitched sounds when they are having fun together.

🐬 Dolphins

Dolphins are very social. They make friends and help each other when someone is hurt.

🦜 Large birds

Parrots and crows are intelligent. They can be generous when a "friend" needs food.

🦒 Giraffes & 🐘 Elephants

Older females act as "babysitters" for babies in the group.

🤝 STRANGE FRIENDSHIPS

Animals can be friends with anyone!

Have you seen videos of strange animal friendships?

🐑 Sheep and 🦆 ducks playing together!
🐕 Dogs and 🐘 elephants!
🐱 Cats and 🐭 mice!

Why does this happen?
When animals don't have others of their own kind, they make friends with the animals they live with. They don't want to be alone.
